Hi, I'm Bastien

I'm a traveler and a web developer


I got a Master's degree in Computer Science in France, before spending a few years working for various startups in France and abroad. My longest mission was with Tilkee, a startup that I joined in its very beginnings as the founding developer and helped grow into a profitable midsized company, eventually taking the role of Lead Developer.

When time came to move on I decided that before committing to another project, I wanted to take a break and dedicate some time to my passion for traveling. Halfway through what was supposed to be a round-the-world trip, I realized that I didn't want to settle anywhere yet. I spent the next 9 years living as a nomad, alternating between periods of full-time travel and of work, including missions with the University of Melbourne and with SingleOps.


Current Situation : I am now hanging up my backpack and settling down back in Europe and am actively looking for a more long-term position, ideally remotely and on a Ruby on Rails stack. Feel free to get in touch!
